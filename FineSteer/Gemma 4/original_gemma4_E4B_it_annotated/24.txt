**Cleaning Methods:**

1.  **Dry Cleaning:** Lightly wipe the screen with a dry and clean microfiber cloth. Do not use paper towels, kitchen towels, or household rags.
2.  **Wet Cleaning:** Mix 1 part isopropyl alcohol and 1 part distilled water in a spray bottle. Spray a lint-free cloth (or LCD cleaner) with this solution and wipe the screen thoroughly in one direction.
