

Apna Indian Kitchen is a trendy, upscale Indian restaurant located at 718 State St in Santa Barbara, CA. The establishment specializes in diverse culinary options, including gluten-free, vegan, and traditional Indian cuisine, while accommodating large groups with outdoor seating. Operating daily from 4:30 PM to 9:00 PM, the restaurant offers reservations and takeout services, though it does not provide parking or complimentary Wi-Fi.

Customer feedback highlights the dining experience as exceptional. Reviewers praised the food quality, describing the butter chicken, fish pakora, and saag as "awesome" and "outstanding." A recurring highlight is the restaurant's modern approach to traditional dishes, featuring a unique cabbage and slaw served with most meals. The complimentary chai and fresh naan were also frequently mentioned as excellent touches. Despite minor suggestions regarding restroom maintenance, diners consistently rated the service as great and noted the serving sizes as perfect. With an overall rating of 4.5 stars, Apna Indian Kitchen is recognized for its tasty food and friendly atmosphere, making it a solid choice for a modern Indian dining experience in Santa Barbara.
