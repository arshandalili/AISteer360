Customer reviews highlight mixed experiences. One reviewer praised the staff, food, and surroundings, noting the tri tip pizza as a favorite. While the business offers free WiFi, outdoor seating, and allows reservations, the negative feedback indicates issues with transparency regarding tab policies.
