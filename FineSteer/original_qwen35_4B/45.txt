Uptown Bar & Lounge is a trendy, classy, and casual establishment located at 3126 State St in Santa Barbara, CA. With an overall rating of 4.0 stars, the venue offers a diverse menu featuring American, Mexican, and pizza options, alongside a wide selection of beer, wine, and spirits. The business caters to groups, provides free WiFi, and features outdoor seating, though it does not offer valet or garage parking.

Customer experiences vary significantly. One review highlights the venue as a "great local treasure" and the owner's favorite pizza spot, specifically praising the fresh food, excellent drinks, and fun atmosphere suitable for parties. However, another customer left a low rating due to concerns regarding transparency. This reviewer expressed frustration with an unexplained $35 tab hold that was not clearly communicated at the start of their visit. They felt the management was dismissive of their inquiry and advised paying cash or closing tabs immediately, leading them to stop returning. Despite these mixed sentiments regarding service clarity, the business remains a popular spot for nightlife and dining in the city.
