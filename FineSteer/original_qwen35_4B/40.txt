

Uptown Bar & Lounge, located at 3126 State St in Santa Barbara, CA, is a trendy establishment offering lounges, American, Mexican, and pizza dining. With a classy and casual ambiance, the venue features free WiFi, outdoor seating, and parking on the street or in a lot. It operates from 12:00 to 1:00 on Saturdays and Sundays, with extended hours on Fridays.

Customer feedback highlights a polarized experience. While some patrons praise the fresh food, accommodating staff, and fun atmosphere—specifically recommending the tri tip pizza and happy hour—others express significant dissatisfaction. A negative review criticizes the lack of transparency regarding a $35 tab hold, noting that the charge was not explained at the start of service and that the staff responded dismissively to inquiries about the policy. Conversely, positive reviews describe the location as a great local treasure where the staff was incredibly accommodating and the food was delicious. Overall, the business holds a 4.0-star rating, reflecting the contrast between its enjoyable environment and specific operational complaints regarding tab management.
