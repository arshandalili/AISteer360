Dining options include takeout and valet parking, though outdoor seating is unavailable. Parking can be found on the street or via the hotel garage for $25 per night. Happy Hour runs daily from 4 PM to 7 PM, featuring discounted small plates, beer, wine, and cocktails. Additionally, Yelp check-ins offer 50% off an item. Despite mixed feedback on specific dishes and drink strength, the restaurant remains a functional dining spot for hotel guests seeking American fare in Santa Barbara. No WiFi is available on-site.
