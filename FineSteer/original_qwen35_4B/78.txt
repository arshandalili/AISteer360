

Located at 1111 E Cabrillo Blvd in Santa Barbara, CA, Bistro 1111 is a casual American restaurant situated within the Hyatt Hotel, offering breakfast, brunch, and new American cuisine. With an overall rating of 3.5 stars, the establishment features an upscale ambiance, valet parking, and is suitable for groups.

Customer feedback highlights mixed experiences. A 4-star review from January 2016 praised the Lamb meatballs and a juicy Bistro burger. The dining experience was noted for attentive service and the availability of a Yelp discount. However, a 2-star review from December 2015 criticized the salmon dish for being dry, not fresh, and containing fish bones. Another 3-star review from November 2015 mentioned tasty Brussels sprouts and crispy wings, though the herb topping and sauces were considered lacking. Additionally, some diners found the drinks to be weak.

Hours are consistent from 6:30 AM to 10:00 PM daily. While the restaurant offers takeout and reservations, it does not have outdoor seating or free WiFi. Happy Hour runs daily from 4 PM to 7 PM with discounted small plates, beers, and cocktails. Street parking is available, though valet service is an option.
