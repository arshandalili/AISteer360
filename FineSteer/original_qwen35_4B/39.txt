

Bistro 1111 is a casual American restaurant located at 1111 E Cabrillo Blvd in Santa Barbara, CA. The establishment offers a classy atmosphere and allows reservations, making it suitable for groups. It is open daily from 6:30 AM to 10:00 PM. While street parking is available, valet service is also provided.

Customer feedback highlights mixed experiences regarding food quality. Some diners praised the lamb meatballs and a juicy brioche burger, noting that the service was attentive. The restaurant also features a daily happy hour from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM, offering discounted small plates, beer, and cocktails. However, other reviews indicate inconsistencies, with complaints about dry salmon dishes, fish bones in the meat, and weak drinks. One reviewer suggested that the Brussels sprouts were tasty, though the herb topping on the wings was lacking. Overall, the venue has received a 3.5-star rating.
