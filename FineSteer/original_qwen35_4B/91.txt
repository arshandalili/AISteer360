Apna Indian Kitchen, located at 718 State St in Santa Barbara, CA, is a highly-rated (4.5 stars) establishment specializing in Indian cuisine, offering Vegan, Gluten-Free, and Gastropubs options. Operating Tuesday through Sunday from 4:30 PM to 9:00 PM, the restaurant offers reservations, takeout, and outdoor seating. While it is suitable for groups, it lacks on-site parking, music, and WiFi. Despite this minor point, the majority of guests expressed satisfaction, with many stating they will return for the tasty meals and friendly staff.
