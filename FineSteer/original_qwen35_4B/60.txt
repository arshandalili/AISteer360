To safely clean your computer screen, follow these steps based on the passages:
1.  **Turn off the laptop** before cleaning to see dust and smudges better.
2.  Use only a **dry, clean microfiber cloth** (never paper towels, kitchen towels, or rags) for the first attempt.
3.  If needed, create a homemade solution of **1 part isopropyl alcohol and 1 part distilled water**, or use an LCD cleaner.
4.  Spray the **cloth** (not the screen directly) and wipe in **one direction** to prevent streaks.
5.  For stubborn spots, do **not rub harder**; instead, use a clean cotton ball or swab on the edges.
6.  Never use window cleaners, abrasive cleaners, or rub directly on the screen, as these can damage it or kill pixels.
