

Located at 137 Anacapa St, Ste C, Riverbench Santa Barbara Tasting Room is a wine bar and tasting room in Santa Barbara, CA, open daily from noon until 5:00 PM. Visitors often praise the knowledgeable service, noting staff member Jordie's passion and detailed guidance during tastings. The establishment offers a variety of wines, including sparkling brut rosé, Pinot Noirs, and Blanc de Blancs, which customers describe as spectacular and phenomenal. Some reviews highlight the unique atmosphere, mentioning a patio with good vibes and the option to customize glittered sparkling wine bottles as a fun holiday gift. Food options are limited to chocolate truffles and small plates like crackers and salami. Parking is available on the street, but there is no WiFi or valet service. While the wine quality and service are highly rated, some guests found the venue lacking in value due to small, uncomfortable seating and limited food choices compared to other local wineries. Overall, the business offers a passionate experience for wine enthusiasts, though opinions vary regarding the ambiance and pricing.
