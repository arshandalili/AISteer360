

Apna Indian Kitchen is a trendy, casual, and classy Indian restaurant located at 718 State St in Santa Barbara, CA. The establishment specializes in a diverse menu including Gluten-Free, Vegan, and Indian cuisine, making it a popular spot for groups. With a 4.5-star rating, the restaurant is known for its excellent service and delicious food.

Customer reviews highlight the quality of the dishes, specifically praising the fish pakora, butter chicken, and fresh naan. Reviewers noted a unique modern twist on traditional dishes, such as cabbage and slaw served with meals, which added flavor and variety. The serving sizes are described as perfect, and the complimentary chai is considered a great touch. While the ambience is rated as hipster and classy, the facilities have mixed reviews; the complimentary chai is a highlight, but the restroom needs some attention. Additionally, while takeout and outdoor seating are available, the venue does not offer parking or WiFi. Reservations are accepted, and the restaurant operates from 4:30 PM to 9:00 PM daily.
