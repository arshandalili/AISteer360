Located at 1417 San Andres St in Santa Barbara, Su Casa Fresh Mexican Grill is a highly rated casual Mexican restaurant with an overall 4.5-star rating. The establishment features outdoor seating and caters to large groups, though it does not offer reservations, takeout, or complimentary WiFi. Parking is available on the street and in a lot, but there is no garage, valet, or validated parking.

Customer feedback highlights the exceptional quality of the food and the friendly service. One reviewer described the experience as finding a "gem" in the neighborhood, praising both the delicious dishes and the staff. Another customer lauded the nachos as "incredible" and noted that the menu accommodates dietary needs, such as low-carb options without rice, which they found healthy and tasty. They also recommended ordering fresh avocados instead of the included guacamole for an extra charge. Additionally, a guest shared a heartwarming anecdote about the owner, Efren from Michoacán, who invited them to stay for dinner and a beer after closing on a slow Monday night, serving them a very tasty plate of Chile Verde. The restaurant maintains consistent operating hours from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM, seven days a week.
