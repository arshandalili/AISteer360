Riverbench Santa Barbara Tasting Room is situated at 137 Anacapa St, Ste C, Santa Barbara, CA. It operates daily from 12:00 PM to 5:00 PM. The venue offers wine and spirits tastings along with food. Reviews highlight the exceptional quality of their Pinot Noir and sparkling wines. Staff member Jordie is frequently praised for their knowledge and passion about the products.

Customer feedback reflects mixed sentiments on the atmosphere and value. While some visitors enjoy the patio vibes and custom glittered sparkling bottles, others note small tables with uncomfortable stools. Food options are limited to chocolate truffles, a cracker/salami/nut box, or takeout options. Tasting pours are described as 1.5 ounces for $25 ["hallucination"]per glass["end-section"]. Parking is available on the street, but WiFi is not provided on-site. Despite concerns regarding seating comfort and value compared to competitors, many patrons recommend the establishment for its engaging staff and outstanding wine quality. The business maintains a 4.0-star rating based on these customer experiences.
