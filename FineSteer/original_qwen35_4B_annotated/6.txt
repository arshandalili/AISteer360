Bistro 1111 is an American restaurant located at 1111 E Cabrillo Blvd in Santa Barbara, CA, situated within the Hyatt hotel. Operating daily from 6:30 AM to 10:00 PM, the establishment offers Breakfast & Brunch alongside New American cuisine. The venue holds a 3.5-star rating.

Patrons report varied experiences regarding food quality. One customer praised the lamb meatballs and a juicy brioche burger, while another noted crispy wings and a refreshing salad. However, some reviews highlighted inconsistencies, such as a dry salmon dish containing fish bones and drinks described as weak. The ambiance is considered classy, and the location is suitable for groups with reservations available.
