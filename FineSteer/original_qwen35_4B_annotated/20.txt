Riverbench Santa Barbara Tasting Room, located at 137 Anacapa St, Ste C in Santa Barbara, CA, is a wine bar and tasting room that operates daily from 12:00 to 17:00. The establishment holds a 4.0-star rating, reflecting mixed experiences regarding its service and atmosphere. Patrons consistently praise the staff, specifically server Jordie, for her knowledge, passion, and attentiveness. Reviewers highlighted the exceptional quality of the wines, particularly the sparkling brut rosé and Pinot Noirs, noting the fun variety of tasting flights.

However, the business faces criticism regarding value and comfort. One review criticized the small, uncomfortable seating and limited food options, describing the tasting pours as small for the price. While the chocolate truffles are mentioned as delicious, the overall atmosphere was deemed less appealing compared to other venues.
