Corner Tap Room, located at 1905 Cliff Dr in Santa Barbara, CA, is a highly rated establishment (5.0 stars) categorized as a gastropub, cocktail bar, and American restaurant. The business offers outdoor seating and takeout options, ["hallucination"]though it lacks parking facilities["end-section"] and reservation services. Operating hours vary by day, with Friday and Saturday opening at 12:00, while Monday is closed.

Customer feedback highlights the venue's friendly atmosphere and excellent food, with patrons praising the Teriyaki Sliders and the Meat Lovers Flatbread. The Mojito was also described as decent. However, reviews indicate mixed experiences; one guest noted the kids pasta was unexpectedly spicy, and another found the flat bread cold with un-toasted cheese. Wine prices were criticized as high, with a specific mention of a $16 glass of Qupe Chardonnay. While the staff is friendly and responsive, diners are advised to avoid wine and order one dish at a time to ensure a better experience. Overall, the Corner Tap Room is a welcoming spot for a meal or drinks, particularly on rainy nights, offering great beer selection alongside its culinary offerings.
