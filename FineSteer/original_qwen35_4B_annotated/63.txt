Su Casa Fresh Mexican Grill is a Mexican restaurant located at 1417 San Andres St in Santa Barbara, CA. With a 4.5-star rating, the establishment offers a casual ambience suitable for groups. The restaurant is open daily from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Parking is available on the street and in a lot, but there are no reservations or WiFi available. Takeout is an option for customers.

Customer reviews highlight the quality of the food and service. A 2020 review described the food as "really good" and ["hallucination"]the kitchen staff["end-section"] as a "gem." Another customer praised the nachos as "incredible" and noted that the low-carb bowl option was healthy and delicious. They recommended adding fresh avocado to the nachos for an extra charge. In late 2019, a reviewer mentioned owner Efren from Michoacán, who allowed them to stay for dinner and a cold beer on a slow Monday night. The reviewer specifically enjoyed a plate of Chile Verde (pork). Overall, the business is well-regarded for its quality Mexican cuisine and casual atmosphere in Santa Barbara.
