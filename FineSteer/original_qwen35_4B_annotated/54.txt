Su Casa Fresh Mexican Grill is a Mexican restaurant located at 1417 San Andres St in Santa Barbara, CA. Holding a 4.5-star rating, the establishment offers casual dining with outdoor seating and accommodates groups. It operates daily from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Guests can park on the street or in a lot, though reservations are not accepted. The venue provides takeout options but does not offer WiFi.
