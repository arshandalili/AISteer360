Uptown Bar & Lounge is located at 3126 State St in Santa Barbara, CA. It operates as a lounge, restaurant, and bar offering American, Mexican, and pizza options. It holds a 4.0-star rating. Operating hours vary, with the venue closed on Mondays and open from 3:00 PM to 11:00 PM on weekdays, extending to 1:00 AM on Fridays and Saturdays. Sunday hours run from noon to 11:00 PM.

Customer reviews highlight mixed experiences. One reviewer praised the staff, food, and surroundings, ["hallucination"]noting the tri tip pizza as a favorite. They described the atmosphere as fun and the drinks as great.["end-section"] However, another customer left a negative review citing an ammonia smell and a confusing $35 tab hold policy that was not explained beforehand. This guest felt the staff was dismissive regarding the charge. While the business offers free WiFi, outdoor seating, and allows reservations, the negative feedback indicates issues with transparency regarding tab policies. The venue is considered classy and casual with music available.
