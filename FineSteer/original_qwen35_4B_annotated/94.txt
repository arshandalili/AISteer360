Pace food+drink, located at 413 State St in Santa Barbara, CA, is a casual American (New) restaurant with a 4.0-star rating. The establishment offers street parking but lacks valet or garage options, and does not support reservations or WiFi. While the venue does not feature upscale or trendy ambience, it is known for its laid-back atmosphere. Customer feedback highlights significant variance in the dining experience. Highly rated reviews from 2014 praise the friendly service and delicious food, mentioning the Caesar salad, Huli Huli Chicken, and micro brews as "AWESOME!!" and "fantastic." Another customer expressed love for the food and atmosphere during a previous visit. However, a review notes the business appears closed. Conversely, a one-star review from late 2014 notes a negative experience, citing a kitchen that closed before the restaurant was out of food and slow acknowledgment from staff. The business operates from 5 PM to 9 PM or 10 PM, Tuesday through Saturday. Despite the positive highlights on food and service in some instances, the review data indicates a sharp contrast in customer satisfaction.
