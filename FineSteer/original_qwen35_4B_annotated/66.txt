Corner Tap Room is a highly-rated gastropub located at 1905 Cliff Dr in Santa Barbara, CA. Operating from 16:00 to 21:00 on Monday through Thursday, 12:00 to 22:00 on Friday and Saturday, and 10:00 to 20:00 on Sunday, the venue offers American cuisine and drinks with outdoor seating and lot parking. It maintains a 5.0-star rating based on customer feedback.

Reviews highlight the friendly staff and excellent beer selection as key strengths. One guest found the Teriyaki Sliders great on a rainy night. Another noted the good location on the Mesa and praised the Meat Lovers Flatbread, though the kids pasta was unexpectedly spicy. A third visitor paid $16 for a glass of Qupe Chardonnay, calling wine prices too high. While the cauliflower dish was tasty, the flat bread arrived cold with cheese not toasted. Despite mixed opinions on specific dishes and wine costs, visitors recommend the location for its atmosphere and service, suggesting ordering one dish at a time to avoid issues.
