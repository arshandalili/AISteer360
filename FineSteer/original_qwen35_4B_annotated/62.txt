Take shorter showers and replace showerheads with ultra-low-flow versions. Fill baths only 1/3 full and use a bucket to catch water while waiting for it to heat. Turn off the tap while brushing teeth or washing hands. Water lawns early morning or evening. Use a broom instead of a hose for cleaning, install a shut-off nozzle on your hose, bathe pets outdoors in areas needing water, and direct downspouts towards garden plants.
