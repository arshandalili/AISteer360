Located at 1417 San Andres St in Santa Barbara, CA, Su Casa Fresh Mexican Grill is a 4.5-star rated Mexican restaurant offering casual dining with outdoor seating. The establishment operates daily from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM. While takeout and group dining are available, reservations are not accepted. Parking is provided on the street or in a lot, though there is no valet or WiFi.

Customer feedback highlights the quality of the food and service. One review praises the incredible nachos, recommending fresh avocado for an extra charge of 1.50. Another customer noted the service was excellent, accommodating low-carb requests. A third review specifically mentions owner Efren from Michoacán, who prepared a tasty Chile Verde (pork) on a slow Monday night after closing. With an overall rating of 4.5 stars, Su Casa Fresh Mexican Grill is described by patrons as a "gem" that they are happy to return to.
