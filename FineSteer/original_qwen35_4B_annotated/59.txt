Le Crepe Shoppe, a French and creperie restaurant located at 15 W Gutierrez St in Santa Barbara, CA, offers a casual dining experience. The establishment provides takeout services but does not accept reservations or offer outdoor seating. Parking is available on the street or in a lot, though garage and valet options are unavailable. With an overall rating of 4.0 stars, customer feedback varies. Positive reviews highlight the freshness and taste of the sweet berry crepes and the strawberry rhubarb crepe, noting friendly service and a quaint shop atmosphere. Conversely, a negative review from a 2012 customer describes a BLT crepe experience that was disgusting. This reviewer cited watery ingredients, buckwheat flour that masked flavors, and lettuce that tasted like pesticides, leading to a gagging reaction.
