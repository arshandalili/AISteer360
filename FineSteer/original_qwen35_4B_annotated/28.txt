Le Crepe Shoppe is a French creperie situated at 15 W Gutierrez St in Santa Barbara, CA. Operating within the categories of French and restaurants, the venue offers a casual ambience. While the business holds a 4.0-star rating, customer feedback presents a mixed picture regarding quality. Positive reviews from 2012 and 2013 describe the food as fresh and tasty, with specific praise for sweet berry and strawberry rhubarb crepes. One reviewer noted friendly service and the quaint nature of the shop. Conversely, a one-star review from 2012 details a negative experience with a BLT crepe. The reviewer claimed buckwheat flour destroyed flavors, ingredients were watery, and lettuce tasted like pesticides, stating they gagged while finishing the meal. Practical amenities include takeout options and parking via street or lot, though outdoor seating and reservations are unavailable. Valet and garage parking are also not provided.
