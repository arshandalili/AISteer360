Located at 15 W Gutierrez St in Santa Barbara, CA, Le Crepe Shoppe operates as a French creperie and restaurant. The establishment offers a casual ambience and permits takeout orders. Customers can utilize street parking, though there are no reservations, outdoor seating, or valet services available. The business holds a 4.0-star rating based on customer feedback. Positive reviews highlight fresh, tasty food, specifically mentioning sweet berry crepes and a delicious strawberry rhubarb crepe. Visitors also praise the friendly service and the quaint shop environment. However, one customer gave a 1-star rating for a BLT crepe. They described the buckwheat flour as destroying flavors and ingredients as watery. This diner found the lettuce coated in pesticides and the experience disgusting. Overall, the shop offers a mix of high praise for sweet items and specific complaints about the BLT crepe.
