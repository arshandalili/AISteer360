Located at 15 W Gutierrez St in Santa Barbara, CA, Le Crepe Shoppe operates as a French creperie and restaurant. The establishment maintains a casual ambience and offers street or lot parking for customers. While reservations are not available and outdoor seating is absent, the business supports take-out orders. The shop has received a rating of 4.0 stars based on customer feedback.

Reviews indicate a mixed experience regarding quality and service. Positive feedback highlights fresh and tasty options, specifically noting the sweet berry crepes and a delicious strawberry rhubarb dish. Customers appreciate the friendly service and the quaint shop atmosphere. However, some reviews are critical. One reviewer expressed strong dissatisfaction with a BLT crepe, describing the buckwheat flour as destroying subtle flavors and ingredients as watery. They noted the lettuce tasted coated in pesticides, leading to a negative dining experience.
