Mels Place is a bar and nightlife venue located at 6 De La Guerra Pl in Santa Barbara, CA. Categorized under Bars and Nightlife, it was established in 1963 and has reopened in a new location off West Carrillo. It is recognized as a local favorite and a dive bar offering a welcoming atmosphere with clean lighting and accommodating staff, notably long-time employee Fran. The venue features a game room equipped with darts, pool, and a jukebox, along with a menu of reasonable-priced eats and drinks. Reviews highlight the strong drinks and value. While it attracts characters and travelers for midday excitement, one reviewer noted the interior can be hot due to limited airflow. Despite a 3.5-star rating, the establishment remains a spot for locals seeking a change of pace away from touristy joints.
