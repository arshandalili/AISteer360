To safely clean your computer screen:
1. Turn off the laptop so it is dark, making dust and smudges easier to see.
2. Use only a dry, clean microfiber cloth (never paper towels, kitchen towels, or household rags). If a cleaner is needed, mix one part isopropyl alcohol and one part distilled water in a spray bottle.
3. Spray the cloth with the solution or cleaner, not the screen directly.
4. Wipe the screen thoroughly in one direction to prevent streaks.
5. For stubborn spots, do not rub harder; use a clean cotton ball or swab for the edges instead.
6. Always ensure the screen is powered off before cleaning.
