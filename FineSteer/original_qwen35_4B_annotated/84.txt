Based on the provided passages, to soak off gel polish:
1.  Soak a cotton ball with acetone remover and place it on the nail.
2.  Wrap the finger with the cotton ball in foil, squeezing it tight to ensure contact.
3.  Wait until the polish visibly loosens at the edges (this process takes 10–15 minutes).
4.  Use an orange stick or cuticle pusher to push the polish off, avoiding anything too sharp.
5.  Repeat for each nail.
