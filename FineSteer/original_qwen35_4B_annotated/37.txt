Based on the passages, tips to conserve water include:

*   **Showers:** Take shorter showers, replace showerheads with ultra-low-flow versions, use units that allow you to cut off flow without adjusting temperature knobs, use a bucket to catch water while waiting for it to heat up, and turn off the tap while brushing teeth or washing hands.
*   **Baths:** Close the drain first and fill the tub only 1/3 full.
*   **Outdoors:** Water lawns in the early morning or later evening in 20-minute intervals, use a broom instead of a hose to remove debris, bathe pets outdoors in areas that need water, install a shut-off nozzle on your water hose, and direct downspouts towards shrubs and trees.
