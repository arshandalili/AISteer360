Turn off the laptop. Use a dry, clean microfiber cloth or a lint-free cloth sprayed with a solution of 1 part isopropyl alcohol and 1 part distilled water. Avoid paper towels, kitchen towels, household rags, window cleaners, or abrasive cleaners. Wipe in one direction. Use a cotton ball or swab for edges. If a spot does not come off, do not rub harder. Always clean the screen powered off.
