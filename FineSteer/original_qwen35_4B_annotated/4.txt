Located at 3126 State St in Santa Barbara, CA, Uptown Bar & Lounge serves American, Mexican, pizza, tacos, and drinks. The venue features a classy and casual ambience with music, outdoor seating, and free WiFi. It is noted as good for groups and offers reservations, takeout, and parking via street or lot. Operating hours vary by day, with Monday closed, and the venue open Tuesday through Sunday.
