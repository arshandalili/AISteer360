Pace food+drink is an American (New) restaurant located at 413 State St in Santa Barbara, CA. It maintains a 4.0-star rating and operates from 17:00 to 21:00 on Tuesday through Thursday, and 17:00 to 22:00 on Friday through Saturday. The venue offers a casual ambience with street parking and takeout available. However, reservations, outdoor seating, and WiFi are not provided.

Customer reviews reflect mixed experiences. Positive feedback highlights friendly service, a laid-back atmosphere, and delicious items such as the Caesar salad and the Huli Huli Chicken. Reviewers also noted great micro brews and group suitability. Conversely, one review criticized slow acknowledgment of guests and early kitchen closure when out of food, noting arrival times near closing. Another customer remarked that the establishment appeared closed for good during a visit.
