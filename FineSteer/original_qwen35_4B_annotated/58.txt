Riverbench Santa Barbara Tasting Room is located at 137 Anacapa St, Ste C, Santa Barbara, CA. It operates daily from 12:00 to 17:00. The business offers wine tasting, beer, wine & spirits, and food. Street parking is available, though there is no garage or WiFi. Customer reviews reflect a 4.0-star rating. Several visitors praised the knowledgeable service, particularly server Jordie, who was passionate and explained wine flights in detail. Popular selections include sparkling brut rosé and Pinot Noirs. One review highlighted the unique offering of custom glittered sparkling bottles for gifts. However, mixed feedback exists regarding the atmosphere and value. One customer noted the tasting pours were 1.5 ounces for $25, the tables were small with uncomfortable stools, and food options were limited to crackers, salami, or truffles. Despite concerns about seating and pricing, many visitors reported an amazing experience. One guest mentioned the venue had great patio vibes and was accommodating for three small dogs. The establishment allows for wine club memberships. Takeout is available for customers.
