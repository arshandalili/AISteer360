Bistro 1111 is an American (New) restaurant located at 1111 E Cabrillo Blvd in Santa Barbara, CA, situated within the Hyatt Hotel. Open daily from 6:30 AM to 10:00 PM, the venue offers breakfast, brunch, ["hallucination"]and dinner["end-section"]. The establishment provides valet and street parking, accepts reservations, and allows takeout. There is no outdoor seating or WiFi available. Ambience is classified as classy.

Customer reviews reflect a 3.5-star rating with mixed feedback. A 4-star review praised the lamb meatballs as delicious and the bistro burger for its juicy patty, noting attentive service from server Edgar. Happy Hour runs daily from 4 PM to 7 PM with small plates at $5 and beers at $4. A Yelp promotion offers 50% off an entree with check-in. However, negative feedback exists. One review criticized the salmon dish for being dry and containing fish bones. Another customer found the drinks weak, though the Brussels sprouts and wings were considered tasty, with some noting the herb topping was lacking. Despite these inconsistencies, the restaurant remains a local dining option in the area.
