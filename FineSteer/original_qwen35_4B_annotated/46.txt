Located at 1905 Cliff Dr in Santa Barbara, CA, the Corner Tap Room operates as a restaurant, gastropub, cocktail bar, and beer bar. It offers American cuisine with a focus on nightlife and traditional American options. The establishment provides outdoor seating and allows takeout, with parking available on a lot. Reservations are not accepted. Hours vary by day, closed Mondays, later on weekends.

Customer reviews reflect mixed experiences. One five-star review highlighted tremendous food, specifically Teriyaki Sliders, and a friendly atmosphere with great beers. Another four-star review praised the location on the Mesa and service, noting the meat lovers flatbread was amazing, though the prosciutto flatbread was disliked. One reviewer also noted the kids pasta tasted very spicy. A three-star review mentioned friendly staff but criticized wine prices, citing a $16 glass of Chardonnay as too high. This review also noted the cauliflower dish was tasty, but the flat bread was cold with cheese not toasted. Patrons suggest ordering one dish at a time to manage expectations. Overall, it holds a 5.0 star rating.
