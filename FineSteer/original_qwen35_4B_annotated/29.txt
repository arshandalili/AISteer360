Based on the provided passages, to plough a field you should mark the center in the front and back with stakes or sticks to establish a straight line, line up the tractor on the stakes, and plow as straight as possible. When you reach the end, turn around and plow back up the field with the first bottom completely overlapping so the middle hill is higher. Line up the wheel in the ditch (furrow) so the first bottom covers the furrow. Note that the method depends on the size, shape of the field, and plow size, and consulting manuals or resources may be necessary depending on the equipment.
