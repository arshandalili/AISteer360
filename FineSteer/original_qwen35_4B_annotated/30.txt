Riverbench Santa Barbara Tasting Room is located at 137 Anacapa St, Ste C in Santa Barbara, CA. Operating daily from 12:00 to 17:00, the establishment holds a 4.0-star rating. Reviews consistently praise the wine quality, specifically the sparkling brut rosé and Pinot noir selection. Staff member Jordie is frequently highlighted for being passionate and knowledgeable, providing detailed explanations and even offering treats for dogs during tastings. Chocolate truffles are also noted as excellent by visitors.

Customer experiences regarding the environment vary. While some patrons enjoy the patio vibes, others mention uncomfortable stools and small tables. Food options are limited to takeout items like crackers, salami, and truffles. Value is a point of contention; one guest found the 1.5 ounces pours for $25 lacking value compared to other locations ["hallucination"]in Santa Barbara Wine Country["end-section"]. Despite mixed feedback on seating and food, the knowledgeable service and wine quality remain strengths. Guests should note that street parking is available, but WiFi is not offered.
