Based on the provided passages, here are tips to conserve water:

*   **Showers:** Take shorter showers, replace showerheads with ultra-low-flow versions, use units that cut off flow without adjusting temperature knobs, and use a bucket to collect water while waiting for it to heat up.
*   **Baths:** Use the minimum amount of water by closing the drain first and filling the tub only 1/3 full.
*   **Faucets:** Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth or washing your hands.
*   **Lawns and Outdoors:** Water lawns in the early morning or later evening in 20-minute intervals, use a broom instead of a hose to remove debris, and bathe pets outdoors in areas that need water.
*   **Garden Hoses:** Install a shut-off nozzle on your water hose and direct downspouts towards shrubs and trees.
