Uptown Bar & Lounge is situated at 3126 State St in Santa Barbara, CA. It operates as a lounge, bar, and restaurant serving American (New), Mexican, Tacos, Pizza, and nightlife options. Hours are Tuesday through Thursday 3 PM to 11 PM, Friday and Saturday until 1 AM, and Sunday 12 PM to 11 PM. Closed Mondays.

The business holds a 4.0-star rating. Customer feedback varies. Positive reviews praise the staff and food quality. One guest described the venue as a "local treasure" with fresh, delicious meals and great drinks during a birthday party. Another highlighted the tri tip pizza as their favorite in town, noting the fun atmosphere.

Conversely, a negative review from December 2021 criticized the experience. The customer reported an ammonia smell and confusion regarding a 35-dollar tab hold that was not explained upon opening. They recommended paying cash or closing tabs immediately to avoid unexpected charges.

The venue offers free WiFi, outdoor seating, and reservations. Parking is available on the street or lot. Ambience is characterized as classy and casual. While some guests enjoy the nightlife and food, others express concerns about billing transparency and hygiene.
