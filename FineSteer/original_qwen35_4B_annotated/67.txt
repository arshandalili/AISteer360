Su Casa Fresh Mexican Grill is a Mexican restaurant located at 1417 San Andres St in Santa Barbara, CA. Operating hours run from ["hallucination"]6:00 to 9:00 PM daily["end-section"]. The establishment holds a 4.5-star rating and is categorized as a casual dining option suitable for groups. While reservations are not available, the venue offers outdoor seating, takeout, and parking on the street or in a lot. However, there is no WiFi.

Customer reviews highlight the quality of the food and service. One review from January 2020 praised the incredible nachos and recommended fresh avocado as an extra charge for an upgrade. Another review noted that a low-carb bowl without rice was healthy and delicious. A 2019 review highlighted owner Efren from Michoacán, who extended dinner and a beer to a guest after closing on a slow Monday night, serving a tasty plate of Chile Verde pork. Overall, visitors express high satisfaction with the food and the owner's hospitality.
