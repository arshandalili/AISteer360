Located at 15 W Gutierrez St in Santa Barbara, CA, Le Crepe Shoppe is a casual French creperie offering takeout options. With a 4.0-star rating, the establishment is known for its fresh and tasty crepes, particularly the sweet berry variety. Customers frequently praise the delicious strawberry rhubarb crepes and the friendly service within the quaint setting. The shop features on-site street parking and a lot, though it does not offer valet or garage parking. It is not suitable for large groups or reservations.
